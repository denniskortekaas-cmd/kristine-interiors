About Kristine

Design With
a Personal Touch

Hi, I'm Kristine. I'm an interior designer with over 10 years of living and working in Dubai, a city I know intimately, from its neighbourhoods and suppliers to the way light moves through its spaces at every hour of the day.

Every project starts with the people who will live in it, their routines, their taste, and what home really means to them. Together with my team, I work with homeowners, expats, and property investors across the UAE to create interiors that are considered, beautiful, and completely personal.
